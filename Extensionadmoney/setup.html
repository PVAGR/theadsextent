How to get Client ID & Secret (3 quick steps) ▸
  1. Go to dev.twitch.tv/console/apps — sign in with your Twitch account.
  2. Click Register Your Application. Name: anything (e.g. "PVA Bazaar"). OAuth Redirect URL: https://localhost. Category: Website Integration. Click Create.
  3. Click Manage → copy Client ID. Click New Secret → copy Client Secret. Paste both below.
