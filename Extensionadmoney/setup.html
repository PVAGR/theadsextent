How to get your Client ID & Secret (3 steps) ▸
  1. Go to dev.twitch.tv/console/apps and sign in with your Twitch account.
  2. Click Register Your Application. Name it anything (e.g. "PVA Bazaar"). Set OAuth Redirect URL to https://localhost. Category: Website Integration.
  3. After saving, click Manage → copy the Client ID. Click New Secret → copy the Client Secret. Paste both below.
