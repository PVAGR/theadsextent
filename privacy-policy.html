Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026 • Version 1.0.0

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how PVA Bazaar / ricktaur ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the PVA Bazaar Stream embed pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Data We Collect

We only collect anonymised, non-personal analytics data for the purpose of academic research and service improvement. Specifically:

  • embed_loaded — timestamp, domain, device type (mobile/tablet/desktop)
  • stream_status_change — whether the channel went live or offline
  • affiliate_click — which product category was clicked (no personal ID)
  • donation_initiated — amount and currency (no payment details)
  • time_watched — estimated session duration in seconds
  • bounce — whether the page was left within 10 seconds

We do not collect IP addresses, names, email addresses, exact geographic locations, or any other Personally Identifiable Information (PII). User session identifiers are one-way hashed (SHA-256) before storage.

3. Your Consent

Analytics are only enabled after you click "Accept" on the cookie consent banner. You may withdraw consent at any time by clicking "Decline" on the banner. If you decline, no analytics events will be sent.

4. How We Use Your Data

Analytics data is used solely for:

  • Improving the streaming experience and embed performance.
  • Academic research into creator-owned streaming infrastructure (PhD thesis, Ethics and Economics of the Creator Economy). All research data is fully anonymised before analysis.

5. Data Retention

Raw event logs are automatically purged after 12 months. Only aggregate statistics (counts, averages) are retained beyond that point.

6. Affiliate Disclosure

This site contains affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate we earn from qualifying purchases. All affiliate links are clearly identified and marked with rel="sponsored". Affiliate clicks are tracked via UTM parameters appended to the URL (utm_source=embed&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=ricktaur).

7. Third-Party Services

This site embeds content from Twitch, which has its own Privacy Notice. Payment processing is handled by PayPal and Stripe, which have their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any data held about you.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (note: our data is anonymised and cannot be linked back to you).
  • Opt-Out — decline analytics tracking via the consent banner.
  • Non-Discrimination — we will never discriminate against users who opt out.

To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@pvabazaar.com

9. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We implement Content Security Policy (CSP) headers, rate limiting on API endpoints, and regular security audits. No sensitive financial or personal data is stored on our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
