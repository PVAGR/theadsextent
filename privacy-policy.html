2. What Data We Collect

With your consent, we collect the following anonymised data for academic research and system improvement:

  • Event name (e.g., "embed_loaded", "affiliate_click")
  • Timestamp of the event
  • Device type (mobile / tablet / desktop) — derived from browser user agent; the raw UA string is discarded

We do not collect: IP addresses, names, email addresses, precise location, or any other Personally Identifiable Information (PII).
